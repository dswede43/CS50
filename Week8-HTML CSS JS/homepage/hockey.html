Hockey

Minor league

Growing up, I played competitive hockey in the greater Toronto area finishing my last year of minor hockey as a defensemen with the Midget AAA Mississauga Rebels. We won the COGECO Golden Horseshoe tournament and finished 2nd place for the 2015-2016 Greater Toronto Hockey League (GTHL) regular season losing in the final round of playoffs.

Sweden

I played one season of hockey for Almtuna IS in Uppsala, Sweden when I was 13 years old living with my uncle away from my parents. I enjoyed playing European style hockey, went to a Swedish elementary school and made friendships in Sweden I still keep today.

High school

In my senior year of high school hockey, our team (the R.H. King Lions) won the Toronto District Secondary Schools Athletic Association (TDSSAA) regional championships in the Scarborough area. I also played for the school field lacrosse team as a long pole, where we lost in the regional finals for our conference.

For my efforts in high school hockey and lacrosse, I was awarded the R.H. King Academy Athletic Award in my last year of high school in 2016.

Junior league

Upon starting my post-secondary studies at the University of Ottawa, I played junior B hockey in the Ottawa area for the Ottawa West Golden Knights. In my rookie year, we finished first in the Tier 2 Central Canada Hockey League (CCHL2) with a record of 46-6.
